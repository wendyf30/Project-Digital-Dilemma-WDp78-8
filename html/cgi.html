The Ethical Challenges of CGI Actors in Films

What Was Digitized? What Was The Goal or Purpose of Digitizing This Thing?
The actors that have passed away have been digitized for use in later films using CGI. The goal of digitizing these actors was to keep the sense of realism in their movies.
Positives and Negatives? Was This Benefitial or Harmful?
Companies benefit from the digitized actors, but the families and fans of the actors usually don't think this is ethical. The CGI actors do allow filmmakers to gain more money, but they may recieve backlash from the viewers.
